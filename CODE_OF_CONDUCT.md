# Contributor Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1, as its code of conduct. The key points are summarised below; the full
text is available at
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>.

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity
and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation. We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open,
welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment include
demonstrating empathy and kindness, respecting differing opinions and experiences,
giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback, taking responsibility and
apologising for mistakes, and focusing on what is best for the community.

Examples of unacceptable behaviour include the use of sexualised language or
imagery and unwelcome sexual attention; trolling, insulting or derogatory comments,
and personal or political attacks; public or private harassment; publishing
others' private information without explicit permission; and other conduct which
could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be
reported to the project maintainers at **[INSERT CONTACT EMAIL]**. All complaints
will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Project maintainers are
obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter of any incident, and
will follow the Contributor Covenant's Enforcement Guidelines in determining the
consequences for any action they deem in violation of this Code of Conduct.
